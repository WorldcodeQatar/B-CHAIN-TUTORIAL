What is Binance Coin (BNB)?


      BNB was launched through an ICO in 2017, 11 days before the Binance cryptocurrency exchange went live. It was originally issued as an ERC-20 token running on the Ethereum network, with a total supply capped at 200 million, with 100 million BNB offered in an ICO.
      BNB can be used as a payment method, utility token for paying fees on Binance Exchange, and participating in token sales on Binance Launchpad. BB also powers the Binance DEX (Decentralized Exchange).


What is Arbitrage?






Arbitrage occurs when a security is bought in one market and sold at a higher price in another, essentially a risk-free trade.
Crypto arbitrage trading is simply buying and selling cryptocurrencies in more than two markets at the same time and profiting from the price difference.
